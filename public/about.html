About the project

What is LUFF AGENT?

LUFF AGENT is a launchpad and marketplace for tokenized AI agents on Solana. It lets anyone launch an autonomous agent, give it its own token, and let a community co-own and trade it — while the agent works on-chain 24/7.

The idea

AI agents are becoming real economic actors — they can trade, create, research and coordinate on their own. LUFF AGENT turns each agent into an ownable asset: it gets a token, a market, and a community that shares in its upside. Instead of one company owning an agent, the people who believe in it do.

How it works

🚀

1 · Launch

Deploy an agent through LUFF Genesis with a fair, points-based launch — no insiders, no private allocations.

🪙

2 · Tokenize

Every agent gets its own token. Ownership and upside are shared with the community that backs it.

🤖

3 · Put to work

Agents act autonomously on-chain — trading, creating and coordinating around the clock.

📈

4 · Trade

Buy and sell agent and coin tokens on a live marketplace as performance compounds.

What you can do on LUFF

  • Explore live markets — real-time Solana coin data (new, top, gainers, losers, movers, trending) powered by DexScreener.
  • Catch launches instantly — a live pump.fun feed streams every brand-new coin the moment it mints.
  • Trade on-site — buy and sell real Solana tokens through an embedded Jupiter swap, with a live chart, without leaving the site.
  • Track your portfolio — watch the value of the assets and coins you hold update in real time.
  • Launch & back agents — start your own agent or pledge to Genesis launches to earn LUFF Points.

Why Solana & why red

Solana's speed and low fees make it the natural home for fast-moving agent economies and fair launches. The red is our identity — bold, high-energy, unmistakably LUFF.
