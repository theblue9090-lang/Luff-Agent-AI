LUFF AGENT Docs

Everything you need to explore markets, trade Solana coins and track your holdings on LUFF.

Getting started

  1. Open the LUFF AGENT app.
  2. Browse Live Markets and the pump.fun Launches feed — no wallet needed to look around.
  3. When you're ready to trade, hit Trade on any coin and connect a Solana wallet inside the swap.

Connecting a wallet

The Connect button in the top bar is a demo connector used for the portfolio and UI state. Real trades use their own wallet connection inside the embedded Jupiter swap (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack and more), so your keys never touch LUFF.

Live markets

Market data is pulled directly from the public DexScreener API in your browser and refreshes automatically. Tabs let you sort the Solana market:

  • New freshly listed pairs
  • Top highest market cap
  • Top Gainers biggest 24h risers
  • Losers biggest 24h fallers
  • Movers highest 24h volume
  • Trending boosted / promoted tokens

pump.fun live launches

LUFF subscribes to a real-time WebSocket (via PumpPortal) that broadcasts every new pump.fun token the instant it mints. Each launch streams into the feed with its logo pulled from the token's on-chain metadata.

Trading & charts

Clicking Trade opens a live DexScreener price chart alongside an embedded Jupiter swap. Use the Buy / Sell toggle to flip the direction between SOL and the token. Swaps execute on-chain through Jupiter's router.

Recommended: set a dedicated RPC

Public RPCs are rate-limited and can block swaps. Copy .env.example to .env and set your own endpoint (Helius, QuickNode, Triton, …):

VITE_SOLANA_RPC=https://your-rpc-endpoint.example.com

Portfolio

The Portfolio section shows the assets and coins you hold with prices that update in real time — total value, all-time P&L, 24h change and per-asset breakdown.

Genesis & Points

Genesis is LUFF's fair-launch mechanism: pledge LUFF Points to help launch the next agent. Trading, pledging and referring friends all earn Points, which convert to allocation in future launches.

FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. LUFF is non-custodial — you interact with a Solana wallet, not an account.

Are the trades real?

Yes. Swaps run on-chain via Jupiter using your own wallet and funds.

Is my money safe?

Crypto is risky and volatile. Verify token mints, start small, and never trade more than you can afford to lose.
